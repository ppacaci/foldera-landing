FAQ

Short answers, clear rules.

What is Foldera?
Foldera explores fixed-term ETH-based structures with clear rules and defined exits. Early access is used to measure interest and shape the structure.
Is this live?
No. Foldera is currently in an early access phase. No funds are accepted at this stage.
How does it work?
Participants indicate preferred terms and amounts. These inputs help define the first structure before anything goes live.
Is there any commitment during early access?
No. Early access does not require payment or commitment.
Which assets are supported?
Initial exploration is focused on ETH. Other assets may be evaluated based on interest.
What happens next?
If sufficient interest is confirmed, the first structure will be finalized and shared with early access participants.
Who is behind Foldera?
The focus is on the structure, not individuals. Foldera is designed as a product-led, anonymous initiative.
Is this an investment offer?
No. This site does not offer investments, guarantees, or financial advice.
